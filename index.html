More About JuiceToast

JuiceToast is proudly developed by the OpenDN Foundation as a modern, lightweight toast notification library. It offers an easy-to-use API, is fully open-source, and completely free to use for developers of all levels. Designed for speed, simplicity, and flexibility, JuiceToast works seamlessly across browsers and platforms, making it perfect for both small projects and large-scale applications. With customizable animations, sound support, and accessibility-friendly features, JuiceToast empowers developers to deliver elegant notifications effortlessly.
